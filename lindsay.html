Lindsay

Christian faith-based counseling for individuals and couples

Areas of focus / Approach

I offer compassionate, professional, evidence-based practices in the context of a shared Christian faith. I would love to offer a space for clients to explore their Christian faith, as they develop a faith that is uniquely their own. We are people requiring holistic care; while religion can be a source of harm, it can also be a source of great joy and healing. I envision a space where Christians can heal in the context of knowing God's love and heart for them. Further, this is a space for Christians to learn how to build boundaried, loving and healthy relationships, meeting our God-given need for community.
