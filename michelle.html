Ecotherapy and nature-based spirituality

Areas of focus / Approach

I offer Nature-Based Spirituality Therapy, also known as Ecotherapy, which integrates the natural world into the healing process to promote emotional, psychological, and physical well-being. By using grounding techniques and fostering inner peace, this approach helps individuals reconnect with themselves and the environment. Through self-reflection and nature-based activities, ecotherapy supports personal growth and healing.

A key component of this therapy includes nature walks and meditation exercises in a forest setting, allowing individuals to engage in mindfulness while benefiting from the calming effects of nature. Research has shown that this approach is particularly effective for individuals struggling with anxiety, depression, trauma, and guilt, as well as those seeking a deeper spiritual connection, whether within or beyond traditional religious frameworks.
