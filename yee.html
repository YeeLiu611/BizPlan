Yee

Individual and Couple Therapy

Areas of focus / Approach

I am a registered clinical counsellor with a passion for helping individuals and couples navigate life's challenges. My therapeutic approach is integrative, drawing from various evidence-based modalities including Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT), Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), and Mindfulness-based approaches.

In my work with individuals, I focus on helping clients develop greater self-awareness, emotional regulation, and coping strategies. I specialize in treating anxiety, depression, trauma, and relationship issues. With couples, I help partners improve communication, rebuild trust, and strengthen their emotional connection.

I believe in creating a safe, non-judgmental space where clients can explore their thoughts and feelings openly. My goal is to help clients develop the tools and insights they need to make positive changes in their lives and relationships.
