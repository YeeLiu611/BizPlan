Sophie

Catholic/Christian integration for couples

Areas of focus / Approach

I specialize in couple therapy, and I am passionate about welcoming spirituality into conversations as I work with couples. As a Christian of the Catholic tradition, I am versed in Sacred Scripture and can offer integration of theology related to marriage while working with Catholic couples and those seeking therapy from a Christian worldview. I see faith as being closely related to culture, and care to journey with couples of varying faiths and cultural backgrounds. I also support relationships where there are inter-faith and inter-cultural dynamics.

My areas of focus are work with dating and engaged couples, work with those looking to re-energize their relationship and to thrive in their marriage, as well as work with couples feeling stuck due to specific challenges. Common relationship challenges I help navigate include, but are not limited to: communication, parenting, family of origin work, sex and intimacy, life transitions, grief and loss. I aim to help couples foster greater connection, safety and love grounded in their own understanding of the Sacred. Therapeutic modalities which inform my work are Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT), Gottman Method Couples Therapy as well as Mindful Self-Compassion. If applicable, I am also able to offer Discernment Counselling for couples discerning a separation/divorce.
